Exercise Counseling and Classes

We help people living with cancer get exercise, both during active treatment and after treatment is over.

Start by signing-up to receive a free one-hour Exercise Counseling session. If you have already had this session then you are ready to sign up for one of our fitness club programs, take our exercise classes, or start following the exercise instructions you received.

Fitness SF Mid Market

One Tenth STreet, San Francisco, CA 94103 (click to view map)

A $25 monthly membership rate at Fitness SF Mid Market is available to new members only. The Sunflower Wellness membership entitles you to the same services and benefits as a regular Fitness SF membership. To set up your Sunflower Wellness membership, you will need to pay with a credit card on our website. Your credit card will be automatically charged each month at the membership rate unless you cancel. Because of the low cost of Sunflower Wellness memberships, Class Passes are not offered. Once signed up is confirmed, contact Fitness SF Midmarket for a tour and arrange to pick up your membership card.
