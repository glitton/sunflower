Exercise and Avoid Inactivity

We help anyone with cancer find a way to get exercise. We are here to help you get moving!

Exercise Counseling

Start here with free exercise counseling.
Either during or after treatment, you'll meet with one of our cancer exercise counselors, who will recommend exercises that are best and safest for you – no matter where you work out, including at home.

Fitness Program Sign-Up

Join Sunflower Wellness at one of our fitness partners located around the San Francisco Bay Area. You’ll receive discounted membership rates, along with access to exercise classes and discounted personal training. Scholarships are also available.

Find Classes

Check out our class schedule. Our exercise classes are taught by fitness professionals who receive specialized training to work with people with cancer. The supportive communities that form in many of our classes will keep you coming back.
