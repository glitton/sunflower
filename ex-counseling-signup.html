Exercise Counseling

What is Exercise Counseling?

Exercise Counseling is a NO FEE service for you to meet with one of our Cancer Exercise Specialists who will provide you with a personalized exercise program. Topics discussed include weight loss or gain, fatigue, range of motion, bone density as well as your health history, exercise background, concerns, and more.
Sign up for Exercise Counseling now.

What Happens After A Session?

For most survivors, the Exercise Counseling session marks the beginning a routine of regular walking, aerobic exercise, weight (or resistance) training, yoga, Pilates, swimming, or rowing. Your program might consist of individual and/or class exercises at a gym, health club and may even involve exercise in the privacy of your home, even movement in bed!

Who are our Exercise Counselors?

Our Exercise Counselors are certified by the American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM) with a Cancer Exercise Trainer (CET) certification. This special designation gives our counselors the skills to adapt many forms of exercise to meet the needs of people living with cancer. Learn more about our exercise counselors.
